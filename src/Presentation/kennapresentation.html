Percentile and Basic Bootstraps

  • I analyzed the variables HighBP, HighChol, BMI, AnyHealthcare, GenHlth, and Income for differences in means among no diabetes, prediabetes, and diabetes.

  • Overall, I found that there was a significant differece in means for all the variables analyzed among the combinations of no diabetes, prediabetes, and diabetes.

  • There was small difference between the CIs for the percentile and basic bootstraps which matched my findings for small bias
